About Us

The ECWA Students Ministry - FUEP Chapter was established to foster spiritual growth and community among students at the Federal University of Education, Pankshin. Rooted in Christian values, we have been serving the campus since our inception.

Our vision is to create a vibrant Christian community that disciples students, promotes evangelism, and supports academic excellence while preparing them for leadership in society.
